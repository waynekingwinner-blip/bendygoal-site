Privacy Policy

BendyGoal: Gymnast Flexibility · Last updated June 30, 2026

BendyGoal ("the app") is a flexibility-training app for gymnasts, dancers, and cheerleaders. We built it to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our servers. The app has no account and no login. Everything you enter — your onboarding answers, workout history, streaks, progress notes, and any before/after photos — is stored only on your device and never leaves it, except through your own device backups (iCloud/Google), which are controlled by you and Apple/Google.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through your App Store account. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives a randomly generated, anonymous app user identifier and purchase/receipt information from Apple in order to verify your subscription. This data is not linked to your name, email, or any personal identity, because the app never asks for those. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy.

Photos

If you add progress photos, they are stored locally on your device only. We never upload them.

Analytics & tracking

The app does not include third-party advertising or cross-app tracking. We do not sell any data.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, no personal data about children is gathered.

Third-party services

Apple (payments & App Store) and RevenueCat (subscription management) are the only third parties involved, and only for the purposes described above.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
