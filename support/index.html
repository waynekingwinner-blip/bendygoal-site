Frequently asked

Do I need to create an account?

No. BendyGoal has no login. Just open the app, answer a few quick questions, and start training. Your progress is saved on your device.

How do I restore my subscription on a new phone?

Open the app, go to the paywall, and tap Restore Purchases. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID that bought the subscription, it will unlock automatically.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → BendyGoal, where you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel. Canceling stops future renewals; you keep access until the current period ends.

Will I lose my progress?

Your progress is stored on your device and is included in your iCloud/device backup. If you delete the app without a backup, or switch between iPhone and Android, local progress may not carry over.
